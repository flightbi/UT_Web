Services

Aviation data modeling services for commercial and network decisions.

UpwardTechnology supports teams that need clean data, defensible analysis, and focused modeling around aviation markets.

DOT Data

Analysis of U.S. aviation data sources for route performance, market size, carrier trends, airport comparisons, and historical context.

02

Flight Schedule

Schedule data processing, frequency review, capacity trends, hub connectivity checks, market coverage, and competitive schedule comparisons.

03

Airline Ticketing

Ticketing data preparation and analysis for market behavior, fare patterns, passenger flows, and route-level commercial questions.

04

MIDT Data Processing

Structured processing of MIDT data into clean outputs for market sizing, origin-destination analysis, connection behavior, and opportunity assessment.

05

Air Demand Forecast and Modeling

Demand forecasting, scenario modeling, and market evaluation for aviation planning, business cases, and strategic decision support.
